Hi, I'm Julius. I work on making language models robust, aligned, and epistemically responsible. I'm a final year undergrad studying **Computer Science** and **Philosophy** at **Georgia Tech**, advised by Prof. Srijan Kumar.

I'm interested in the relationship between humans and AI systems, and how we make sure it goes well on both ends. My research interests are in (1) **adversarial robustness**, studying how safety training and guardrails fail in practice; (2) **AI alignment**, particularly **pluralistic alignment** and **preference representation**, understanding and improving how models learn values and preferences; and (3) **human-AI interaction and coevolution**, studying how AI shapes what people know and believe and how we can best work with models. 

Previously, I was a ML engineering intern at LinkedIn on the Job Seeker Experience team, and before that I did an internship at Microsoft AI on the Copilot Infrastructure team.  Outside of research, I like playing the drums, listening and looking for new [music](https://www.last.fm/user/juliusdcreator), grappling, playing [chess](https://www.chess.com/member/juliusdcreator), and (unfortunately) Rainbow Six Siege. Don't hesitate to get in touch — I'm always happy to hear from people!
