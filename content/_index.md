Hi, I'm Julius. I work on making language models robust, aligned, and epistemically responsible. I'm a final year undergrad studying **Computer Science** and **Philosophy** at **Georgia Tech**, advised by Prof. Srijan Kumar.

I'm interested in the relationship between humans and AI systems, and how we make sure it goes well on both ends. I work on **adversarial robustness**, studying how safety guardrails fail in practice; **pluralistic alignment** and **preference representation**, studying how models learn values and preferences and whose values they end up serving; and the **human-AI coevolution**, thinking about how AI shapes what people know and believe. 

In my free time, I like playing the drums, looking for new [music](https://www.last.fm/user/juliusdcreator), grappling, and playing [chess](https://www.chess.com/member/juliusdcreator). I'm always happy to chat about research, collaborations, or anything else — please feel free to reach out!
